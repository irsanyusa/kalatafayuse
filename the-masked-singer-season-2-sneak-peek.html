The Masked Singer released a new sneak peek for season two. And it reveals six never-before-seen crazy costumes we’ll see this fall! From animals to plants to food, it’s going to be a wild one.

Watch the video below then let’s break down the new costumes.

Before we get into it, we have already revealed a handful of the costumes we’ll be seeing on season 2 of The Masked Singer – go check them out. And we’ve talked about everything you need to know for the The Masked Singer Season 2. So read through those if you haven’t yet. We’re only going through the new additions here!

The Masked Singer New Costumes

The Spider

Because we only got about a second to see each costume in this sneak peek, we have to go off of what we have. The Spider’s costume is incredible. The “eyelashes” on it and its figure make me think that this is almost for sure a female. She also appears a little more on the thicc side, if you will. Maybe it could be an athlete?

Thingamajig

Thingamajig is basically…Monster 2.0 in a mismatch plaid suit jacket? After last season’s Monster won the whole thing, maybe it’s not a bad idea to follow suit. Except this time around, he’s much more slim and has super long arms. At least that’s true from this sneak peek! I’m thinking this has to be someone tall. Could it be an athlete or another rapper paying homage to T-Pain last season?

The Flower

The more that I look at The Flower, the more it creeps me out. I like the idea but what is that mask? Either way, I’m assuming this is a woman, based on the flower crown and color choices. We don’t see much of this costume, but it looks like there’s plenty of glitter and glam too! Plus, it’s nice to see some unique costumes other than the predominately animal ones we saw last season.

The Tree

Another out-of-the-box costume here, The Tree! This one is absolutely ridiculous, adorned with bright tinsel and ornaments. It just SCREAMS comedian to me, but it really could be anyone. Also what is that mouth? There’s a 100% chance I’ll have nightmares about this costume. But it’s also kind of fun? I’m confused.

The Ice Cream Cone

There has been so much hype about The Egg costume, but how has The Ice Cream Cone been overlooked? It looks so fun and happy. Though there’s not a very clear look at this costume, the details on the mask look perfectly kitschy and cutesy. It’s hard to tell if this one is a male or female because it could honestly go either way. But no matter what, it looks like this will be an upbeat performance from The Ice Cream Cone!

The Dog

FINALLY! My favorite animal is represented. The Dog looks gangster, with a big fur jacket, Nikes on his feet, and a backwards hat. This costume definitely gives me rapper vibes, whether it’s just this song choice or a clue about the celebrity underneath. It would honestly be fun to see someone do a rap song in general, whether they have experience in it or not. That could bring the competition to the next level!

To get you even more excited, The Masked Singer posted a video of a few season two contestants doing the Git Up Challenge.

The Masked Singer Season 2 Cast and Premiere Date

We’re just over a month away from the season premiere! The Masked Singer returns to FOX on September 25th. It will be hosted once again by Nick Cannon with the three judges Nicole Scherzinger, Jenny McCarthy and Robin Thicke.
