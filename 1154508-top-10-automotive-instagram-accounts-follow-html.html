Top 10 automotive Instagram accounts to follow

Publish date: 2024-08-02

You already use Instagram to look at your favorite people and things, whether it’s members of your family, adorable pets, iconic fashion brands, scrumptious food accounts, or stunning travel photography. Why not add a dash of amazing automotive shots to your feed? Here are 10 must-follow automotive Instagram accounts for every car lover!

1. Formula 1 (@f1)

Formula 1 is one of the most popular topics among the Internet car fans, and what is a better place to check out all things Formula 1 than their official Instagram account? Here you’ll find the most memorable events, new high-tech rides, racing stars, and everything in between!

2. Toyota UK (@toyotauk)

Toyota has local Instagram accounts in every country where it’s represented, but few of them are as impressive to look at as the UK-based account. See classic and brand new cars, learn the latest Toyota news, and stay in touch with your favourite car brand!

3. Alex Penfold (@alexpenfold)

Alex Penfold is a London-based photographer who often travels for work. Anywhere he goes, Alex loves to discover supercars casually parked in the street or chilling in a parking lot. There are also plenty of mesmerizing staged shots of the most expensive cars in the world.

4. Petrolicious (@petrolicious)

Petrolicious is an online car magazine that is a daily source of inspiration for anyone who is passionate about cars. Dazzling car shots, interviews with important personalities from the automotive world, and regular clips and films about beautiful cars - what’s not to like?

5. PanchoMacho (@panchomacho)

Knowing the love of Nigerians for fast and good-looking motorcycles, we simply had to include at least one account dedicated exclusively to motorcycles in all of their rugged, retro, or luxury glory!

6. Honda Tuning (@hondatuning)

If you’re not just interested in stock versions of your favourite Honda models, but also want to know how to get more out of them, this is the Instagram feed for you to follow. See what the best tuned Hondas look like from the outside and on the inside!

7. Exotic Cars & Supercars (@exotic_performance)

An Instagram account that only features the most exotic and expensive cars in the world currently has over 2.5 million followers who can’t wait for new shots of Porsche, Ferrari, Lamborghini, Aston Martin, McLaren, Bentley, and other luxury cars to show up in their feed.

8. Retro Cars (@retro.cars)

In case you’re in a mood for some classic models that are 40, 50, or 60 years old, then make sure to follow this account! Retro cars from the world’s most caring owners in beautiful settings - this Instagram will always inspire you!

9. Mercedes-AMG (@mercedesamg)

Regular Mercedes-Benz cars already look stunning and are featured all over the Instagram, but if that’s not enough for you, follow the official account for the high performance Mercedes-AMG lineup of cars, which already has over 6.7 million followers.

10. RM Sotheby’s (@rmsothebys)

Who knows more about the world’s most exclusive and rarest cars than the most famous auction? See retro models and new supercars that are currently on sale! Who knows, maybe one of them will become yours one day?
