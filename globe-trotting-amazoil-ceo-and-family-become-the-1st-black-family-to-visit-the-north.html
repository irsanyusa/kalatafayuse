Amazoil CEO and family become the 1st black family to visit the North Pole

Publish date: 2024-08-19

The North Pole, also known as the Geographic North Pole or Terrestrial North Pole, is the point in the Northern Hemisphere where the Earth's rotation meets its surface. It should not be confused with the North Magnetic Pole.

The North Pole is the northernmost point on the Earth, lying diametrically opposite the South Pole.

The adventure began with with Og Amazu skydiving 10-000 ft over the North Pole, then scuba diving 10M below the ice.

In 1981 Amazoil Nigeria Ltd was formed to market oil and gas products sourced mainly in Nigeria to global oil trading corporations.

Og Amazu is currently working on a project called The Challenge 100 which aims to fund and support 100 inspiring projects in Nigeria, Britain and across the world that will change lives forever.

At the moment they have committed to support 8 projects and are looking for 92 more.
