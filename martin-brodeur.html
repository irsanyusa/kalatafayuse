Martin Brodeur (NHL Goalie) - On This Day

Publish date: 2024-07-13

Profession: NHL Goalie

Biography: Played his entire career with the New Jersey Devils, and is highly regarded as the greatest goaltender of all time. In his 21-year tenure with the Devils, he has won three Stanley Cup championships. He also won two Olympic gold medals with Team Canada in the 2002 and 2010 Winter Olympic Games.

Born: May 6, 1972
Birthplace: Montreal, Quebec, Canada
Age: 52 years old

Generation: Generation X
