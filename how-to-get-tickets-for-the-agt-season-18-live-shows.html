America’s Got Talent is gearing up to start the Season 18 live shows on August 22, and fans can request tickets to appear in the audience. The best part is that tickets are completely free, with dates currently available through the end of August.

AGT Live Show Tickets Are Available Now

Tickets for the AGT live shows are available through On Camera Audiences. The first week of shows will take place on Tuesday, August 22, and Wednesday, August 23. (Tuesdays are performance nights, and Wednesdays are results nights.)

The shows will take place at Pasadena Civic Auditorium in Pasadena, California. Currently, tickets are available through the end of the month. Dates for September will have tickets available soon. You can sign up to be notified when they’re available.

Each listed show has two arrival times, and audience members are asked to choose only one. This is to keep lines down at the theater. According to the website, seat assignments will be distributed equally at each arrival time.

In a press release, On Camera Audiences shared that this season’s audience experience includes more giveaways and photo opportunities, including photos at the judges’ desk and on the red carpet.

We Still Have More Auditions to See

The live shows are coming up soon, but we still have more auditions to see before then. We’re also expecting two more Golden Buzzers — one from judge Sofia Vergara, and one group Golden Buzzer from the judges and host.

Last week, the show took a week off from new auditions and instead showed a special called “Simon’s Most Memorable Auditions.” The judge counted down his top 16 choices for the most unforgettable auditions from both AGT and BGT.

This week, we’re back to new auditions for Season 18. Are you excited about the live shows coming up? Do you plan to request tickets? Let us know in the comments below.
