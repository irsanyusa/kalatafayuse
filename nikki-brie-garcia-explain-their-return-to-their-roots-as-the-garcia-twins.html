Nikki, Brie Garcia Explain Their Return To Their Roots as The Garcia Twins

Publish date: 2024-07-09

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by Theo Wargo/Getty Images

Reading Time: 3 minutes

In a recent appearance on Live with Kelly and Mark, former WWE wrestling sensations, Nikki and Brie Garcia, revealed an exciting change in their lives – they are now officially known as the Garcia Twins. This transformation marks a significant shift from their iconic wrestling personas as “The Bella Twins,” and they shared the reasons behind this decision.

The Garcia Twins Reveal The Reason Behind Their Return

The Garcia Twins, as they are now known, explained that the change was inspired by a desire to reconnect with their heritage and embrace their true selves. Nikki elaborated on this decision, saying, “Let’s go back to our roots, Mexican and Italian.”

Kelly Ripa, one of the show’s hosts, couldn’t help but express her admiration for their decision, emphasizing the beauty of celebrating their Mexican and Italian heritage. It’s clear that the Garcia Twins are embracing their cultural backgrounds and using this change as a way to embark on a new chapter in their lives.

As they approach their 40th birthday in November, Nikki and Brie are enthusiastic about the next phase of their lives, one that focuses on entrepreneurship and motherhood. Nikki expressed her excitement about this new journey.

“We are entering this new chapter, about to be 40 in November,” Nikki said. “So we thought, you know, entering this next decade of our lives, let’s go back to our roots as entrepreneurs and mothers.”

The decision to shed their wrestling moniker and return to their real surname, Garcia, reflects a profound personal and cultural journey for Nikki and Brie. This change not only allows them to embrace their heritage but also signifies a new beginning filled with exciting opportunities and adventures as they enter their 40s.

Will The Garcia Twins Return To Wrestling?

Following Nikki and Brie Garcia’s transition from their wrestling personas as The Bella Twins to their real names, speculation has arisen about their future in the wrestling world. The Garcia Twins, instrumental in the women’s wrestling revolution, shifted their focus to motherhood and entrepreneurship after retiring from professional wrestling. However, recent developments have left fans curious about the possibility of their return.

In a surprising twist, the Garcia Twins symbolically passed the torch to another set of twins, Haley and Hanna Cavinder, who signed a WWE NIL (Name, Image, Likeness) deal in 2021. This touching moment, shared on social media, has sparked speculation about the Garcia Twins’ potential involvement in WWE, even if not as active competitors.

While it’s unclear if the Garcia Twins have retired from wrestling altogether, their transition to embracing their heritage and new identities as entrepreneurs and mothers shows that they are excited to explore new horizons. As fans eagerly watch their journey, one can’t help but wonder if the Garcia Twins will ever grace the wrestling ring once more, even if only for a special moment that pays homage to their iconic legacy.
