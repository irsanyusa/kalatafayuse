The Masked Singer judge Jenny McCarthy posted the perfect response to Kylie Jenner’s new blood-soaked photo shoot this week, and she didn’t even have to utter a single word for it to be absolutely hilarious.

Jenner posted the photo to promote her new Nightmare on Elm Street cosmetics collection, but it predictably inspired a lot of period jokes. That’s also the direction McCarthy took for her own response.

Jenny McCarthy Jokes About Kylie Jenner’s Bloody Photo

In Jenner’s new photo, she sits naked on the floor, covered in fake blood, with even more fake blood on the floor around her. McCarthy’s TikTok response shows her strolling past the photo. After a moment, she pulls a tampon out of her jacket pocket and holds it out as if offering it to Jenner.

McCarthy captioned the funny video, “and I thought I had heavy flow days.” She also reposted the clip on her Twitter account, writing, “It happens to all of us eventually.”

And she’s not the only one to make a period joke about Jenner’s new photo. Plenty of other social media users had the same thought, with one writing, “When you sneeze on your period.” Others thought it was “disturbing” since Jenner is currently pregnant with her second child.

What Is Jenner Promoting with Her Bloody Photo Shoot?

You might be wondering why Jenner is getting her photo taken covered in blood. It’s all to promote her new Nightmare on Elm Street makeup collection for Kylie Cosmetics, inspired by Freddy Krueger himself.

The collection features lip lacquers, an eyeshadow palette, false lashes, and a lip and cheek stain. The packaging pays tribute to the horror film, with blood splatter, slash marks, and even a cartoonish rendering of Krueger’s scarred skin.

The collection will launch October 12 at 3 pm PST on the Kylie Cosmetics website. Unfortunately, it doesn’t look like tampons are available.
