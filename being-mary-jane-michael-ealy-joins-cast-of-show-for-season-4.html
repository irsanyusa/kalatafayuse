Michael Ealy joins cast of show for season 4

Publish date: 2024-08-02

According to a report by Deadline, Ealy joins the show as Justin Talbot, the new producer at Great Day USA who is also the man that got Mary Jane fired from CNN.

“Being Mary Jane” premiered on January 7, 2014, starring Gabrielle Union as Mary Jane Paul, a successful cable news anchor who has a closet packed with designer clothes and shoes, a beautiful home, a nice car, and the drive to achieve even greater heights.

As Mary Jane looks to start a family of her own to share life's greatest gifts, she discovers who she really is and the people who help define her.
