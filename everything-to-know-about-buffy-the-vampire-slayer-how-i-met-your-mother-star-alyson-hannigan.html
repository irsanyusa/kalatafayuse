Everything to Know About 'Buffy The Vampire Slayer', 'How I Met Your Mother' Star Alyson Hannigan

Publish date: 2024-07-25

ABC/Andrew Eccles

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Alyson Hannigan, beloved actress known for her iconic roles in Buffy the Vampire Slayer and How I Met Your Mother, is set to take center stage once again, this time as a contestant on the upcoming season of Dancing with the Stars. Let’s delve into everything there is to know about this talented star and her exciting journey on DWTS Season 32.

Alyson Hannigan Rose To Fame Following a String of TV, Movie Appearances

From a young age, Alyson exhibited a natural talent for acting, which eventually led her to the pinnacle of television stardom. Alyson’s breakout role came with her portrayal of Willow Rosenberg in the hit TV series Buffy the Vampire Slayer, where she enchanted audiences with her wit, charm, and relatable character.

Following her success on Buffy, Alyson continued to captivate viewers with her role as Lily Aldrin in the long-running CBS sitcom How I Met Your Mother. Her performance as the nurturing yet quirky character solidified her status as a television personality.

Alyson also made a significant impact on the big screen. She is most notably recognized for her portrayal of Michelle Flaherty in the American Pie film series, a character that became an integral part of the franchise.

Most recently, Alyson made her mark as the charming host of Penn & Teller: Fool Us, a magic competition series where magicians aim to mystify the renowned magician-comedian duo, Penn Jillette and Teller. Alyson stepped into this role during the show’s third season in July 2016, bringing her charisma and engaging personality to the forefront.

She Kept Her DWTS Involvement as a Secret

Alyson’s journey on DWTS Season 32 was a secret ahead of the cast announcement. The actress revealed that she kept her casting on the show a well-guarded secret, even from her closest friends. Her commitment to maintaining this surprise reflects her determination to succeed in the competition.

“Like, I didn’t even tell one of my best friends,” Alyson said. “I just texted her last night saying Watch GMA tomorrow. I am a rule follower, I haven’t told anyone.”

Fans of Alyson are in for a delightful surprise as she teams up with professional dancer Sasha Farber to showcase her dancing skills and compete for the coveted Mirrorball trophy.

Joining Alyson in the DWTS Season 32 cast are a diverse array of celebrities, including Barry Williams from The Brady Bunch, Matt Walsh from Veep, Tyson Beckford, Mira Sorvino of Romy & Michele’s High School Reunion fame, and singer Jason Mraz. This season of DWTS premieres on Tuesday, September 26 simultaneously on ABC and Disney+.
