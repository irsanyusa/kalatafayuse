Jada Pinkett Smith admits she had a relationship with August Alsina

Publish date: 2024-06-20

Pinkett sat down with her husband, Will Smith for the latest episode of her show 'Red Table Talk' where she talked about the relationship with Alsina.

"I guess about four and a half years ago I started a friendship with August. We became really really good friends and it all started with him needing some help and me wanting to help his health and mental state," she said.
