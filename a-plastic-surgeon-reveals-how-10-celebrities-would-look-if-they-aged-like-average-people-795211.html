A Plastic Surgeon Reveals How 10 Celebrities Would Look If They Aged Like Average People

Publish date: 2024-09-06

In the world of celebrities, time really works differently — no matter how old they are, they always look beautiful, young, and fresh. That’s why a cosmetic surgeon from London, Dr. Julian De Silva, decided to figure out what famous Hollywood stars would look like now, if they aged like “average women.” To do this, he used his Future Face software to analyze 10 old photos of celebrities and add some normal signs of aging that could have appeared on their faces over the course of 20 years.

We, at Bright Side, were surprised by the results that Dr. De Silva got and we’d like to share them with you.
