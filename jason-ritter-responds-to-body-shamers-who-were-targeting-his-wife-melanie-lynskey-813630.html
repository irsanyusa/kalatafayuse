Jason Ritter Responds to Body Shamers Who Were Targeting His Wife, Melanie Lynskey

Publish date: 2024-05-26

Jason Ritter and Melanie Lynskey are actors known for their roles in movies and TV shows. They are talented in their careers and a loving couple who always support each other. Recently, some people online said mean things about Melanie’s body, but Jason stood up for her and defended her against the trolls. We want to share more about their adorable relationship and why they make such a great couple.

Jordan Strauss / Invision / AP / East News

Like many other Hollywood couples, Melanie and Jason first met while working on a movie together. It happened in 2013 when they were filming The Big Ask. Since then, they have been fortunate to be cast in several projects alongside each other. They even had the opportunity to portray siblings in the film We’ll Never Have Paris. Their on-screen collaborations have allowed their relationship to grow and flourish.

Jason’s proposal to Melanie was absolutely adorable.

Melanie and Jason had a relationship for four years until 2017 when Jason proposed. The proposal was a complete surprise to Melanie, as she had no idea it would happen. Jason chose a casual setting on their sofa to pop the question. In an interview, Melanie simply described it as “cute,” leaving it at that.

Their family expanded and grew a little bit more.

In 2018, just a year after their engagement, Melanie and Jason joyfully welcomed a baby girl into their lives. Melanie shared the happy news on her social media, expressing their gratitude for being able to keep it a secret among close friends and family for some time. However, the news eventually got out, prompting her to confirm and share their excitement.

Since then, the actress has regularly posted adorable pictures of their daughter with her doting dad. In one photo, she captioned it with affection, describing their little girl as knowing she has the best dad in the world. Melanie also spoke about their teamwork in parenting, “He’s so helpful, so supportive, and he’s willing to travel with me so that the family can be together,” she said. “I do the same for him, but I’ve been working a lot recently, so he’s been making the sacrifice more often than I have.”

The couple had a rushed wedding.

Jason and Melanie opted for a small, intimate wedding with just six people in attendance. The reason behind their rushed ceremony was a misunderstanding. Someone misinformed Melanie that Jason needed to be married to enter Canada for her TV show filming in Vancouver, which turned out to be untrue, as revealed by the actress.

With this false information, Melanie panicked and felt compelled to get married immediately, exclaiming, “We have a child, what? We have to get married tomorrow.” Their wedding took place on their front porch, officiated by a kind woman Melanie called a “nice lady.” The guest list included two close friends and, of course, their daughter.

Melanie has faced unwarranted comments about her appearance throughout her career.

Melanie has faced challenges, including hurtful comments about her weight from colleagues and online. She vividly remembers a particular incident while filming Coyote Ugly when the costume designer made insensitive remarks, saying, “’Nobody told me there would be girls like you.’ Really intense feedback about my physicality, my body.”

Jason stood up for Melanie and defended her against online trolls.

Invision/Invision/East News, Invision/Invision/East News

In early 2022, Melanie spoke out about the negative comments regarding her body that some people made online. She expressed her frustration with the backhanded remarks, where people would pretend to be concerned about her health. In response, the actress highlighted, “You don’t see me on my Peloton! You don’t see me running through the park with my child. Skinny does not always equal healthy,” a sentiment that resonates with many.

Even Melanie’s husband, Jason, supported her by posting a clever message on his social media. He wrote, “If anyone has any further unsolicited comments about *anybody* else’s body, they can feel free to write them in permanent ink onto their own foreheads and swan dive directly into the sun.”

The couple consistently provides support for one another.

Jason serves as Melanie’s personal cheerleader when it comes to her career. He supports her by helping with auditions and providing encouragement when they face challenges or feel nervous about a scene. Jason admires Melanie’s talent and considers her a fantastic actress who inspires him.

In an interview, when asked about her husband, Melanie expressed, “I honestly feel like I won the lottery, I have the greatest husband, he’s such a good dad, he loves me, he makes me feel beautiful no matter what, he’s a good, good person, and he’s so cute.” partnership.

Their anniversary

Melanie Lynskey recently celebrated her anniversary with her husband, Jason Ritter, by sharing a heartfelt post on social media. In the caption, she expressed her joy and love, addressing Jason as a wonderful, kind, and beautiful man. Melanie playfully acknowledged the limited number of event pictures, sharing one with her followers.

Celebrities have increasingly taken a stand against body shamers, using their platforms to defend their wives and promote body positivity. One notable example is the beloved actor Tom Hanks, known for his talent and genuine personality. When faced with unwarranted criticism towards his wife’s appearance, Hanks did not hesitate to speak out in her defense.
